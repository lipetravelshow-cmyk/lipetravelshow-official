What kind of Europe fits your trip?

Use these travel angles to refine your view before deciding on country, city, and itinerary combination.

1) First time in Europe

A strong option for travelers who want to start with more classic and easy-to-combine destinations such as Portugal, Spain, France, or Italy.

2) Urban and cultural Europe

A strong fit for travelers who want major cities, museums, iconic neighborhoods, gastronomy, and a more intense rhythm.

3) Historic and scenic Europe

It makes sense for travelers who want castles, medieval centers, scenery, and routes with more atmosphere.

4) Nature and contemplative Europe

Ideal for travelers who prioritize mountains, lakes, panoramic trains, and more visual journeys.
