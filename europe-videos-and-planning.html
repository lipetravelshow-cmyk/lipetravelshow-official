What kind of Europe fits your trip?

Use these lenses to refine your view before locking in a country, city or route combination.

1) First time in Europe

A good fit for travelers who want to start with more classic and easy-to-combine destinations such as Portugal, Spain, France or Italy.

2) Urban and cultural Europe

Ideal for big cities, museums, iconic neighborhoods, gastronomy and a more intense pace.

3) Historic and scenic Europe

Best for castles, medieval centers, landscapes and routes with more atmosphere.

4) Nature and contemplative Europe

Ideal for mountains, lakes, panoramic trains and visually driven travel.
