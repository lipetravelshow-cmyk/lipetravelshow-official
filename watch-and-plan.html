How I like to structure this journey

From video to the real trip

I would rather not send you straight into a cold search. First comes inspiration. Then context. Only after that do flights, hotels and planning tools come in more naturally.

How this journey works

How I think about the path

1. Inspiration: you arrive through a video, a playlist or the memory of a destination that made you want to experience it for yourself.
2. Context: then you land on a page that translates that inspiration into something more practical: area, trip style, pace and stay logic.
3. Planning: only then do flights and hotels appear, at the point where the decision starts to make real sense.
Why I wanted to build this bridge

Why this makes the site feel more alive

I want the site to speak more directly with the channel. Instead of feeling like a static directory of pages, it becomes a more natural editorial journey: you get inspired by the content, understand the destination better and only then move into the practical layer.

What comes next

Next destinations to deepen

China already has a strong foundation. Rome, New York and Buenos Aires have started to build that bridge between content and planning. Lisbon makes perfect sense as the next step, and Spain also opens a major opportunity to expand this logic in a stronger way.
