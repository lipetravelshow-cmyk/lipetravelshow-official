Your method

From emotional pull to real decision — without skipping the middle layer

My way of structuring this journey is simple: desire first, context second, planning third. That sequence makes the practical side feel more intelligent and less generic.

1. Inspiration
You arrive through a video, a memory or the feeling that a destination keeps echoing after you watched it.
2. Context
The page translates that desire into something clearer: region, stay logic, pace, style and timing.
3. Planning
With a more mature decision, flights, hotels, experiences, insurance and AI tools enter at the right time.
