Asia is the continent that challenges me the most as a traveler and creator. Each country has a completely different logic — connectivity, mobility, food, language and pace. That's why, for every destination on this page, the commitment is to deliver real context, not generic attraction lists.

I started with China because it's the Asian destination with the most touchpoints with Brazil today — and because I went deep in multiple trips, with a web series and a project with Embratur and CICG. Other destinations will come when the content is mature enough to be genuinely useful.
