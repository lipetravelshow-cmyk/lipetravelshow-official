🇧🇷   Brazil is having a moment

Brazil: videos, mood and planning

A lot of countries have beautiful beaches, mountains, canyons and dramatic nature. What makes Brazil unforgettable goes beyond scenery: its people, warmth, joy, rhythm, food, cultural confidence and an energy that is hard to fake. This page is my way of turning that feeling into an editorial gateway — part inspiration, part context, part next step.

Why this page matters

Brazil has momentum — and real emotional pull

Brazil closed 2025 with a record 9.3 million international visitors and 103.0 million domestic trips. For me, this page also carries a personal layer: receiving Brazil’s National Tourism Award in the Press & Social Media category reinforced my desire to present the country with more depth, desire and clarity.

What Brazil delivers best

More than postcard beauty

Brazil can be joyful, layered, warm, urban, beachy, musical, soulful and nature-rich — often all at once. That is the version of Brazil I try to show in the channel: a country where scenery matters, but people and energy matter just as much.

What stands out to me most

Why travelers fall for Brazil

People: the human warmth changes the entire texture of the trip.
Joy & brasilidade: there is a rhythm and emotional openness that gives Brazil its own signature.
Range: beaches, cities, rainforests, gastronomy, history, music, islands, dunes, mountains and cultural depth all live inside the same country.
How I would use this page

From the video to the trip

I would start with repertoire. Watch by region, notice what pulls you in emotionally, and only then move into flights, hotels and route logic. That order usually creates much better travel decisions.
