Eastern Europe • Prague & Czech Republic

Prague & Czech Republic: a fairy-tale Europe of beer, castles and urban charm that is hard to beat

Prague is one of Eastern Europe’s great gateways because it translates the region’s imagination so well: towers, bridges, the astronomical clock, trams, cobbled streets, beer and a beauty that feels almost scenic. But the Czech Republic grows further once castles, spa towns and the historic interior enter the picture.

Why it is so compelling

Prague is one of Europe’s most desired cities for a very clear reason

Prague brings together medieval city fabric, an iconic bridge, beer, literary atmosphere, classical music and a historic center of enormous visual impact. It also speaks strongly to travelers who love trams, cafés, photogenic winters and Christmas markets.

What it evokes

Urban fairy tale

It feels like a city built to leave visual memory behind. Its appeal is immediate, strong and extremely consistent.

To go further

The Czech Republic does not end in Prague

Karlovy Vary, Český Krumlov, castles and the historic interior expand the trip with great elegance.

Uma das extensões mais bonitas para quem quer aprofundar o imaginário medieval.

Karlovy Vary

Castelos

A camada de castelos ajuda a reforçar a sensação de Europa central de conto de fadas.

Cerveja & cafés

Praga funciona muito bem para quem gosta de cidade vivida, boa mesa e atmosfera.

Closing note

A very strong gateway into Eastern Europe

Prague & the Czech Republic work brilliantly for travelers who want to begin through the most visual, most photogenic and most immediately charming side of the region.
